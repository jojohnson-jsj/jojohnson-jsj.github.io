About me

I'm a Senior Honors Scholar studying Computer Science
at the University of Central Florida. Originally from Cuba,
my family and I moved to the states when I was 4 years old.
My dad worked on computers back on the island, and he showed
me from early on that computers are powerful tools that
can turn ideas into reality. Since then, I've spent
my time on a few different robotics teams and following and
staying informed on tech of all sorts, from the newest
Google Pixel leaks to Boston Dynamic's Spot robot.
